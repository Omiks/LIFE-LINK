Main Goal:

Give students a short but eye-opening game about time-management and the importance of a balanced life. Our game offers a unique and calming atmosphere where students can take a break by exploring negative and ideal approaches to common situations that an average student can face in their scholarly career.

Why did we choose to make a game?

When we were thinking about the theme: "educational challenges", we had numerous different ideas, but the common idea we all agreed on was: Stress. Stress is one of the main educational challenges that students face. It leads to burnout, mental pressure, and physical deterioration. Many students use outside sources, like games, reading, or doing hobbies to comfort themselves. We do our best to reflect these situations presented in our game.
